4-DOF / 6-DOF Robotic Arm

ROS2 · ros2_control · MoveIt 2 — Motion Planning & Control

A full ROS2 software stack for a 4-DOF/6-DOF arm, from inverse kinematics through collision-free trajectory planning to closed-loop execution.

ROS2 MoveIt 2 ros2_control Inverse Kinematics Gazebo
Architecture
Structured nodes, controllers, and hardware interfaces to support modular motion planning and execution.
Kinematics
Implemented forward and inverse kinematics to convert end-effector poses into joint-space commands.
Key work
  • Configured MoveIt 2 with workspace collision objects for safe, repeatable motion
  • Integrated ros2_control with joint controllers for closed-loop trajectory execution
  • Validated the full pipeline in Gazebo on Linux before hardware deployment
◆ Tuned controllers to resolve planning failures and smooth trajectories
